Privacy and safety

Privacy Notice

How My Literacy Hub handles educator and learner information.

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Information we use

Educator accounts use a display name and email address. Educators create learner accounts using aliases and system-generated sign-in credentials. The hub stores reading progress, activity responses, metacognition responses, points, and group membership needed to provide the learning experience.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate users, save learning progress, show learners their work, and help educators support learners in their own groups. The hub is not designed for public rankings, advertising, or selling personal information.

Learner privacy

Educators should use learner aliases and must not enter sensitive personal information. Learner accounts cannot register publicly and are managed by their educator.

Service providers

The website is hosted through GitHub Pages. Authentication and application data are handled through Supabase. These providers process limited technical information required to operate and secure the service.

Security and retention

Access controls are used to separate educator groups and learner records. Information is retained while an account is active or as needed to operate the service. No internet service can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

Educators may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of their account information. Requests concerning a learner account should be made by the educator responsible for that learning group.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to myliteracyhub@gmail.com.
