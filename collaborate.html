Collaborative Literacy Discussions

Share ideas, listen carefully, and build meaning in a respectful space.

I can use a sentence starter to respond to an idea.

Agree and Add

“I agree with ___ because ___. I would also add ___.”

Ask for Evidence

“What part of the text helped you think that?”

Respectfully Disagree

“I see it differently because the text says ___.”

Turn and Talk

Which character showed courage? What evidence supports your idea?

30-Second Response

Begin with “My idea is…” and add one reason or text clue. Pauses are okay.
