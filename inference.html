Example

Passage: Leo saw dark clouds covering the sky. He carried his soccer ball inside and called his teammates.

Text clue: Leo carried his soccer ball inside after seeing dark clouds.

What students may already know: Dark clouds often appear before it rains, and soccer is usually played outside.

Possible inferences: Leo thinks it may rain (Correct) · Leo lost his soccer ball · Leo does not like his teammates
